FC BARCELONA

Marc-André ter Stegen

Ter Stegen has been playing at the top level of the game since the age of 19. Excellent with the ball at his feet, he is a player for the present and the future

Lionel Messi

Leo Messi is the best player in the world. Technically perfect, he brings together unselfishness, pace, composure and goals to make him number one.

Emerson Royal

Quick right back who also supports the attack

Sergiño Dest

Right sided defender who likes to get forward but is quick to get back into position. His pace makes him a difficult opponent for attackers.

Óscar Mingueza

A strong ball-playing central defender

Jordi Alba

As quick as anyone, Jordi Alba is recognised as one of the most attacking left backs in modern football

Ronald Araujo

Uruguayan center-back with excellent potential, good at bringing the ball up the field and skilled in the aerial game.

Frenkie de Jong

His versatility, energy and great vision have allowed him to continue developing his skills in any midfield position

Clément Lenglet

Considered one of the most promising defenders on the planet, the Frenchman stands out for his ability to bring the ball out and his reading of the game

Gerard Piqué

Considered one of the best centre halves in the world, Gerard Piqué brings together character, class and strength in the air at centre back, something that has made him a key figure in the Barça team

Sergi Roberto

Sergi Roberto is one of the most versatile players in the squad and is known for his ability to get forward and his creativity

Samuel Umtiti

The French international, at the Club since 2016, is known for his physical strength, his ability to anticipate, and his prowess in getting the ball up the field.

Eric García

Centre back who excels at getting the ball out from the back and has an instinctive ability to read the game

Sergio Agüero

One of the finest strikers on the planet, who contributes goals, character and experience

Antoine Griezmann

Griezmann is a very mobile player, with the ability to combine with teammates or make individual plays, while also helping out at the defensive end by bringing pressure
